I am a mathematician and my areas of interest include commutative algebra and algebraic geometry and their applications to hyperplane arrangements, statistics and computer science. Graham Denham was my PhD supervisor at the University of Western Ontario.

Computations

I'm currently working on the practical aspects of machine learning as well as certain conceptual connections between statistical methods in machine learning and various topics in commutative algebra and combinatorics. In partiuclar, I'm interested in graphical associahedra and their connections to Bayesian/Markov networks, hidden Markov models and so on.
